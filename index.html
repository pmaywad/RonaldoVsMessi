Messi vs Ronaldo

Who is the best player?

 Source:   Kaggle


Never in the history of football had been a rivalry like that of Leonel Messi and Christiano Ronaldo. Below are two charts depicting the performance of both the players for seasons from 2009 to 2018.


LA LIGA

Messi, playing for FC Barcelona, has more goals, assists and assists per match compared
to Ronaldo in La Liga in more number of seasons.

Rhetoric Techniques used in the above visualization are:

  • Omission: Filtering the data to only La Liga statistics for the players in this chart.
  • Metonymy: Have selected the metrics where Messi's stats are more for more
    number of seasons in La Liga.
  • Typographic mappings: Have assigned red color to Messi and Blue color to
    Ronaldo. In addition to this coloring the bar between two points with the color of
    highest scrorer.

CHAMPIONS LEAGUE
